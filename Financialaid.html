Big Dreams Financial Aid

Big dreams has partnered with Vivinaa Enterprise to support the African youth by making available financial aid to African students in order for them to complete their desired academic courses.



The requirements needed to qualify for the Big Dreams financial aid include:

  • A monthly payslip for working prospects
  • Students of all academic levels, i.e, primary school, high school, college, masters, etc. with a consistent high grade of A,B or C
  • All prospective students should be Africans
  • Special cases

Based on the stated criteria and necessary information we lend loans to qualifying applicants.


For students who struggle to save while embarking on their educational journey,
we provide an Investment or savings option
as a means of assistance
