Big Dreams Scholarship and Financial Aid

Financial Aid and Scholarships requirements

The Big Dreams Scholarship and Financial Aid aims to assist well-performing students of all academic levels in Africa who struggle to fund their educational journey.

Requirements

  • Students of all academic levels, i.e, primary school, high school, college, masters, etc. with a consistent high grade of A,B or C
  • Determined well-performing students located in rural areas
  • All prospective students ahould be African
  • Special cases

Types of Aid we offer

  • Scholarships
  • Student Loan
  • Best-performing scholar award
  • Investment/Savings option for students


For more information about Special cases and more, kindly reach out to us via the contact details provided on the website
