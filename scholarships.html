Big Dreams Scholarships

Big dreams aims to contribute to the African space by supporting young and determined African students to complete their desired academic courses.

One way we plan to achieve this is by offering scholarships to deserving students.

The requirements for earning a scholarship with Big Dreams include:

  • Students of all academic levels, i.e, primary school, high school, college, masters, etc. with a consistent high grade of A,B or C
  • Determined well-performing students located in rural areas
  • All prospective students should be Africans
  • Special cases
