About Us

At Big Dreams we are on a mission to assist well-performing students of all academic levels in Africa who struggle to fund their educational journey.

We are located in 13 countries all over the world. Our headquarters is located in Accra, Ghana.

The types of Aid we offer

Scholarships

Our scholarships are awarded to African stdudents
who meet our stated criteria

Student Loan

Based on the stated criteria and necessary
information we lend loans to qualifying applicants

Best-performing scholar award

This award is given to the best performing
student on our scholarship award list

Investment/Savings option for students

For students who struggle to save while
embarking on their educational journey,
we provide an Investment or savings option
as a means of assistance.

Special Cases

This category is for students who,
for one reason or another, do not qualify
for the options stated above.

For more information about Special cases
and more, kindly reach out to us via
the contact details provided on the website
